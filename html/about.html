about

Hi everyone. I'm a Portuguese living in Germany who spends the free time outside taking pictures or in the darkroom developing them. Currently I only use analog photography as it is what makes me feel confortable but, at the same time, passionate. One of my biggest interests is to create books/zines focused on storytelling. Through them I show you a little bit of myself and my particular view of the world. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me via e-mail. It is always a pleasure to have the opportunity to connect with people interested in photography as well.
Hope you enjoy my work!
